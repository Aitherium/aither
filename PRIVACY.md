# Privacy Policy — AitherOS (Alpha)

**Effective: March 1, 2026**

---

## ⚠️ Alpha Privacy Disclaimer

AitherOS is in **ALPHA**. During this phase, there are **NO guarantees of data privacy or security**. This policy describes our intentions and best-effort practices, but they are subject to change and may not be fully implemented at all times.

If you require guaranteed data privacy or compliance with specific regulations (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, etc.), **do not use this service until it exits alpha**.

---

## 1. What We Collect

We aim to collect as little data as possible. During the alpha phase, the following may be collected:

- **Server logs** — IP addresses, request timestamps, user agents (for debugging and abuse prevention)
- **Conversation data** — prompts submitted through the chat interface (used exclusively for debugging and system improvement)
- **Account information** — email and display name if you register
- **Error reports** — crash data generated by the application

**We do NOT:**
- Sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties
- Run advertisements
- Use third-party analytics trackers

---

## 2. Local-First Architecture

AitherOS is designed to run entirely on your own hardware. When self-hosted:

- All data stays on your machine — no cloud telemetry, no phone-home
- AI models run locally via Ollama — your prompts never leave your network
- No API proxying through Aitherium servers
- You control all data retention, backups, and deletion

**This is a core architectural principle, not just a feature. Your data is yours.**

---

## 3. Demo Instance (demo.aitherium.com)

The demo instance at demo.aitherium.com is a **SHARED environment** hosted on Aitherium infrastructure:

- Anything you input (messages, prompts, uploaded files) may be stored on our servers
- Conversations may be visible to the system operator for debugging purposes
- Data may be shared with AI models (local Ollama instances) for processing
- The demo may be reset, wiped, or taken offline at any time without notice
- **Do NOT input sensitive, confidential, or personally identifiable information into the demo**

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The demo instance may use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Cloudflare** | DNS, CDN, tunnel for demo.aitherium.com | Standard web traffic metadata |
| **GitHub** | Repository hosting, authentication | Public repo data |
| **Ollama** | Local AI model inference | Runs on Aitherium hardware — no external data sharing |

Self-hosted instances do not use any Aitherium-operated third-party services by default.

---

## 5. Data Retention

During the alpha phase:

- There is **no guaranteed data retention period** — data may be deleted at any time
- Server logs are typically retained for 30 days, then automatically purged
- Conversation data may be retained indefinitely for development purposes, or deleted without notice
- Account data is retained until you request deletion or the alpha period ends

To request deletion of your data, contact us at the email below.

---

## 6. Cookies & Local Storage

The Service uses minimal browser storage:

- **Authentication tokens** — stored in cookies/localStorage to maintain your session
- **UI preferences** — theme, layout, and tour completion status stored in localStorage
- No third-party tracking cookies
- No advertising cookies
